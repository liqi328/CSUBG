DataSet:

The original protein-protein interaction data of S.cerevisiae, consisting of 4950 proteins and 21,788 interactions, was downloaded from the DIP database (2009, version12)(http://dip.doe-mbi.ucla.edu/) Static_network.txt

The gene-expressing profiles of S.cerevisiae were retrieved from Tu et al., 2005, which contains 6777 gene products and 36 samples in total, with 4,858 genes involved in the yeast PPI network. series_matrix.txt
